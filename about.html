Our Story

VoltTracker was born from a simple frustration: EV owners were missing out on thousands of dollars in legitimate tax deductions because manual mileage tracking is tedious, error-prone, and easy to forget.

As early Tesla adopters and small business owners, our founders experienced this pain firsthand. They were driving for business daily but losing money at tax time because they couldn't prove their business mileage to the IRS.

The solution became clear: leverage the advanced connectivity of modern electric vehicles to automatically capture every business mile with IRS-compliant precision. No apps to remember, no logs to maintain - just automatic savings.
