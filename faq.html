Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about VoltTracker

VoltTracker connects securely to your electric vehicle's API (starting with Tesla) to automatically detect when you start and stop driving. Our system:

  • Monitors your vehicle's location and status in real-time
  • Automatically logs trip start/end times and locations
  • Uses GPS data to calculate exact mileage
  • Categorizes trips based on destinations and patterns

No phone app required - it works completely in the background through your vehicle's built-in connectivity.

Currently supported vehicles:

  • Tesla: All models (Model S, 3, X, Y, Cybertruck)
  • Coming Soon: Ford Mustang Mach-E, F-150 Lightning
  • Coming Soon: Rivian R1T, R1S
  • Coming Soon: BMW iX, i4

We're rapidly expanding support for more EVs. If your vehicle isn't listed, join our beta and let us know - we prioritize based on user demand.
